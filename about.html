About us

Welcome to real meal, your partner on the journey to a healthier, more vibrant you. We are a premier meal plan company based in the UAE, fueled by a team of expert chefs dedicated to crafting delicious and nutritious meals tailored to your wellness goals.

At real meal, we believe that achieving your health and fitness aspirations begins with what you put on your plate. Our culinary team, comprised of seasoned experts, meticulously curates meals that not only tantalize your taste buds but also nourish your body. We understand that each person's journey is unique, and our mission is to provide a personalized and enjoyable path to success for every individual.

About us

Our commitment extends beyond the kitchen; we aspire to empower every single person to achieve their goals. Whether you're looking to fuel your workouts, maintain a balanced lifestyle, or embark on a transformative wellness journey, real meal is here to support you.

Real meal is more than just a meal plan company; it's a holistic approach to health and well-being. Join us in making your wellness goals a reality, one delicious and nutritious meal at a time. Your journey starts here, with real meal – where your goals become our mission.

Our mission

To empower individuals to lead healthier, happier lives by providing convenient and delicious meal plans crafted with a commitment to nutrition, variety, and sustainability. We believe that everyone deserves access to wholesome, flavorful meals that nourish the body and inspire a lifelong love for healthy eating. Through our dedication to quality ingredients, expert culinary craftsmanship, and a customer-centric approach, we aim to make wellness attainable and enjoyable for all, fostering a community that celebrates the joy of nourishing the mind and body.

Convenience

Making it easy for people to eat healthy by taking the guesswork out of meal planning and preparation.

Deliciousness

Creating meals that are not only nutritious but also flavorful and enjoyable to eat.

Nutritional balance

Ensuring that meals are packed with essential nutrients to support overall health and well-being.

Freshness

Using only the freshest, locally sourced ingredients whenever possible.

Quality

Maintaining the highest standards of quality in every aspect of the business, from sourcing ingredients to preparing and delivering meals.
